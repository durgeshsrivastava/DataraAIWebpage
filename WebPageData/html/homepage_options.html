How It Works
The Sim-to-Real gap, solved.
Three-step pipeline that turns raw factory footage into high-precision robot training data.
Step 01
🎥
Capture Exocentric Video
Source existing factory cameras (EXO) — no new hardware needed. BMW line, warehouse AMR footage, data center assembly.
Step 02
⚙️
RoboEyeView Conversion
Patented pipeline removes human technician, generates 3D rendering, outputs robot-eye-view egocentric dataset with physics-aware conditions.
Step 03
🚀
Train. Deploy. Dominate.
Higher accuracy (>95%), smaller inference models, faster Sim-to-Real transfer. Foundation model companies and robot OEMs use DataraAI data directly.
