How It Works
EXO — Exocentric
External Camera View
Third-person footage from fixed factory cameras — overhead or wall-mounted — observing the robot and workspace from outside.
→
EGO — Egocentric
Robot's-Eye View
First-person perspective the robot sees. The viewpoint required for training accurate robot policies — costly and hard to capture at scale directly.

🎥
